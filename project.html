About Reverse Prodigy Reverse Prodigy is an alias for electronic musician and producer Neelabh Raj. Reverse makes atmospheric dance music that sonically bridges the gap between indie pop/alt indie pop and electronic music. At the mere age of 18, his debut release "I Miss You Now" on Merchant Records garnered 1 million plays across all platforms. He also worked on movie score for Woh Khaas which will be aired on Disney Hotstar, Sony Liv, MX Player and youtube.
